ICI COMPUTER INSTITUTE

ICI Computer Institute was founded in the year 1999. ICIT is a part of ICI Education Pvt.Ltd . Institute is one of the leading software training companies in India offering comprehensive IT courses and cutting-edge training programs to IT aspirants [students & individuals], working professionals, corporates, institutions and government entities.

Since its inception it has provided quality education to more than 30 plus Center and more than One Lac students for various certifications programs including Govt. Certification and Industry Certifications. ICI is recognized by Govt. and ISO 9001:2015 Certified center. It is also recognized byNIELIT(formally known DOEACC Society) (Ministry of Information Technology, Govt. of India, New Delhi).

ICI is the also Associate with Tilak Maharashtra University, Pune, Manipal University, Jaipur andDr.D.Y.Patil, Pune. ICI EDUCATION PVT.LTD. is a VTP Centre of MSSDS i.e.(Maharashtra State Skill Development Society) The society is functioning under the Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Department, Government of Maharashtra.

ICI is not only providing education in classroom / practical lab but it is also entered in the corporate training. e.g. Mumbai Police Department, Akashwani, Bank of Baroda, Prime Data Systems, Indian Airforce etc.
